xboxController is Controller 1, port 0. 
xboxControllerTwo is Controller 2, port 1.
Double check above in Drivestation.

xboxController is used for driving, utilizing mostly the left thumbstick.
Bumpers/Trigges are used to 'yaw' the robot.

xboxControlelr is the fire control group. Left joystick Y raises and lowers the elevator. 
Right joystick Y increases/decreases pitch. 
Triggers power flywheels
